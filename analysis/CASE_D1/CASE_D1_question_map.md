# CASE_D1 Question Map

## Scope
CASE_D1 (Day 1 — Childhood). Seven discussion questions structured in two parts.

## Question structure

### Part 1 — Wellbeing concept and pillars

| Q# | Question text (Arabic) | Question text (English) | Focus |
|----|----------------------|------------------------|-------|
| Q1 | ما معنى الحياة الطيبة بالنسبة لك؟ | What is wellbeing for you? | Open conceptualisation of wellbeing |
| Q2 | كيف تعرّف العافية من منظورك الشخصي؟ عندما نقول هذا الشخص "بعافية" ماذا نقصد؟ | How do you define wellness from your perspective? When we say someone is "well," what do we mean? | Wellness definition, dimensions |
| Q3 | بناءً على ركائز HT، كيف يمكن أن تسهم هذه الركائز في تحسين حياتك الطيبة؟ ما الاقتراحات التي يمكنك تقديمها؟ | Based on HT pillars, how can these pillars contribute to improving your wellbeing? What suggestions can you provide? | Pillar-based improvement, suggestions |

### Part 2 — Reality, challenges, opportunities, and action

| Q# | Question text (Arabic) | Question text (English) | Focus |
|----|----------------------|------------------------|-------|
| Q4 | ما هو واقع الحياة الطيبة في مجتمعنا خاصة بالنسبة للأطفال؟ | What is the reality of wellbeing in our society especially for children? | Current state assessment for children |
| Q5 | ما التحديات التي تواجه الحياة الطيبة للأطفال؟ | What are the challenges facing children's wellbeing? | Barriers, obstacles, systemic issues |
| Q6 | ما الفرص المتاحة لتحسين الحياة الطيبة للأطفال؟ | What opportunities are available to improve children's wellbeing? | Opportunities, existing resources |
| Q7 | ما اقتراحاتكم العملية لتحسين الحياة الطيبة للأطفال؟ | What are your practical suggestions for improving children's wellbeing? | Concrete recommendations, policy actions |
